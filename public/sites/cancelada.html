1 Public information

Two adjoining cadastral parcels forming an urban enclave in Urbanización Riviera Beach, Cancelada (Estepona) — coastal plain immediately south of the A-7, about 450–500 m from the Saladillo shoreline, surrounded by rústico land (polígono 5 of Cancelada). One parcel is vacant; the smaller carries an unfinished 1987 commercial shell (~7,120 m² built).
