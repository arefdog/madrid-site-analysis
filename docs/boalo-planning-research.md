## 1. Planning regime in force  — confidence: HIGH

- El Boalo-Cerceda-Mataelpino ("BoCeMa") has **no PGOU**. It runs on the **Normas Subsidiarias (NNSS-97)** — original 1996/97, revision definitively approved **BOCM 28 Aug 2001** — patched by point modifications since.
- The town **abandoned drafting a new Plan General**; the region's *Territorial Impact Report of 27 Nov 2018* found "fundamental defects" and directed it toward **densification without increasing buildability** ("urban surgery").
- NNSS land classes: **suelo urbano · suelo urbanizable (apto para urbanizar) · suelo no urbanizable protegido** (agrícola, forestal preservado, PRCAM, vías pecuarias).
- Rough totals cited: **~409,099 m² urban · ~427,237 m² urbanizable**; **zonas verdes cession ~25%**.

Sources: [Ecologistas en Acción – expansionismo/opacidad](https://www.ecologistasenaccion.org/206085/), [Normativa Urbanística – Ayto.
